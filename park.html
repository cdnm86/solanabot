Croatia's oldest national park

Sixteen lakes, endlessly falling into each other

A chain of terraced lakes in impossible shades of turquoise, joined by travertine waterfalls and wooden walkways that float just above the water. The park is open 365 days a year — and Villa Mukinja sits a ten-minute walk from Entrance 2, closer than any hotel car park.

    Hours & Tickets

    When the park runs

    • Open 365 days a year — entrances 07:00–20:00 in summer, shorter off-season
    • Ticket sales end at 17:00; parking open 07:00–21:00
    • Seasonal prices: roughly €10 winter · €23.50 shoulder · €40 summer per adult
    • Buy online in advance in high season — entry slots sell out
    • Train & boat rides are included in the park ticket

    Local Knowledge

    Tips from your hosts

    Beat the crowds

    Enter before 9:00 or after 15:00 — tour buses own the middle of the day in summer.

    Start at Entrance 2

    It's a ten-minute walk from the villa, with the calmest morning queues and direct Upper Lakes access.

    Good shoes, no drone

    Boardwalks get slippery; sturdy soles help. Drones and swimming are not allowed in the park.

    Fuel up first

    Add our home-made breakfast the evening before — food inside the park is scarce and pricey.

    Sleep next to the lakes

    Wake up ten minutes from Entrance 2

    Stay at Villa Mukinja and walk into the park before the buses arrive — then come home to the sauna and hot tub.

Wellness & Spa

Rest, restore, repeat

After a day exploring waterfalls and forest trails, there is no better way to recover than warm water and gentle heat. Our wellness facilities are free for every guest — no booking, no extra charge.

Outdoor hot tub

The Sundance Maxxus 880

One of the largest hot tubs you'll find at a guesthouse — your key to ultimate relaxation under the open sky.

  • Over 50 hydrotherapy jets
  • Ozone therapy for clean, fresh water
  • Aromatherapy for a deeper sense of calm
  • Sun loungers and a private garden setting
Finnish sauna

Warmth that does you good

Step into our authentic Finnish sauna to expand your wellness experience at Villa Mukinja.

  • Soothes and relaxes tired muscles
  • Supports natural detoxification
  • Wonderful for skin and circulation
  • Natural-wood cabin with a calm atmosphere
Slow evenings

The art of doing nothing

Some of the best moments at Villa Mukinja happen after the hiking boots come off.

Pair a soak in the hot tub with a glass of Croatian wine, watch the light fade over the treetops, and let the pace of the park slow you right down. This is what a Plitvice stay should feel like.

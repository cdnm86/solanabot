The Plitvice Valleys

The park is just the start

The lakes and waterfalls will take your breath away — but the surrounding valleys are packed with adventure. Here are some of our guests' favourites, all a short drive from the villa.

Deer Valley Ranch

A magical 15-hectare outdoor zoo home to over 100 game animals you rarely get to see in real life.

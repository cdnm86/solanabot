Dobrodošli — Welcome

A warm Croatian welcome in the heart of the park

Run by the same family for over forty years, Villa Mukinja was built on a simple promise: comfortable, affordable and all-around exceptional hospitality.

Our team does their utmost to make your stay fun, relaxing and memorable — from cosy interiors and genuinely comfortable beds to a breakfast our guests still write home about. You are not just booking a room; you're settling into a complete experience, moments from one of the most beautiful places on earth.

  • A 10-minute walk to the Plitvice Lakes entrance
  • Free Finnish sauna & outdoor hot tub for every guest
  • Home-cooked breakfast & dinner with local specialties
  • Free private parking and fast Wi-Fi throughout

Eat & Drink

Breakfast to die for, dinners to linger over

Our kitchen cooks honest local cuisine from the freshest ingredients — and the welcome doesn't stop at the door.

Mornings begin with the regional dairy specialty basa and home-made cheese. Evenings unfold over a four-course dinner, a carefully chosen Croatian wine — a Dingač or an Istrian Malvazija — and a glass of house plum brandy (šljivovica), traditionally on us.

Beyond the Lakes

Adventures on your doorstep

The national park is only the beginning. From wildlife to white water, the Plitvice valleys are full of things to do.

Deer Valley Ranch

A 15-hectare outdoor zoo home to over 100 animals rarely seen up close.

Zipline Pazi Medo

1,700 m of pure adrenaline — one of the longest ziplines in Europe.

Adrenalin Park OGI

Rope courses, axe throwing, paintball and karting for the whole family.

Una River Rafting

Springtime white-water rafting on one of Croatia's most beautiful rivers.

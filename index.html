Our Story

A Legacy of
Croatian Hospitality

Ever since we opened our doors in the 1980s, we have had one goal in mind: offering our guests a comfortable, affordable, and all-around exceptional experience in the heart of nature.

Villa Mukinja is named after a type of tree that grows exclusively in this area, whose fruits are a real treat for the brown bears that roam these ancient forests. Just like the mukinja tree, we are deeply rooted in this land.

Our family-run villa covers every aspect of your stay — a central location within the national park, cozy beds, delicious homemade food, and superb services that make you feel at home.

Wellness & Spa

Taste of Croatia

Homemade
Croatian Cuisine

Every morning begins with a hearty breakfast featuring Basa, a beloved local dairy specialty, alongside our homemade cheeses and fresh seasonal produce from the region.

In the evening, savour traditional Croatian dishes prepared with love and local ingredients. Each dinner is accompanied by a complimentary glass of our homemade šljivovica (plum brandy) — a cherished Croatian tradition and our warm welcome to you.

Discover

Explore Plitvice Lakes

Step outside your door and into one of Europe's most spectacular natural wonders. Plitvice Lakes National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is just a 10-minute walk away.

Plitvice Lakes National Park

UNESCO World Heritage Site with 16 cascading lakes connected by waterfalls, surrounded by ancient beech and fir forests.

Hiking & Walking

Miles of well-maintained trails through pristine forests and along crystal-clear lakes.

Wildlife & Nature

Home to bears, wolves, eagles, and rare butterflies. A paradise for nature enthusiasts.

Scenic Viewpoints

Breathtaking panoramic views of the lakes, waterfalls, and the surrounding Dinaric Alps.

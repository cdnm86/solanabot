Dobrodošli — Welcome

A warm Croatian welcome in the heart of the park

Run by the same family for over forty years, Villa Mukinja was built on a simple promise: comfortable, honest and all-around exceptional hospitality.

Our team does their utmost to make your stay fun, relaxing and memorable — from cosy interiors and genuinely comfortable beds to a home-made breakfast our guests still write home about. You are not just booking a room; you're settling into a complete experience, moments from one of the most beautiful places on earth.

  • A 10-minute walk to the Plitvice Lakes entrance
  • Free Finnish sauna & outdoor hot tub for every guest
  • Home-made Croatian breakfast, freshly prepared on request
  • Free private parking and fast Wi-Fi throughout

Eat & Drink

A breakfast worth waking up for

Add our home-made Croatian breakfast to your stay and mornings become a highlight of their own.

Freshly prepared each morning on request, breakfast stars the regional dairy specialty basa and our own home-made cheese — honest, hearty fuel for a full day among the waterfalls. Later, slow down on the garden terrace with a carefully chosen Croatian wine: a full-bodied Dingač or a crisp Istrian Malvazija, poured as the light fades over the treetops.
